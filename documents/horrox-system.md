# Horrox System

Astronomical designation: m.471

The planet Horrox was discovered by astronomer Horaccio Roxas using the
Star Terminal.

## Planet Horrox

A rocky exoplanet several light‑years from Earth.

Features: - Mineral‑rich crust - Multiple moons - No confirmed
biological life

Horrox became the primary target for LightTech exploration missions.

## Moon: Minerva

Minerva was selected as the first mining site.

Characteristics: - Rich in Hydronium Crystallite - Heavy meteor impact
history - Extensive crater fields

Mining operations on Minerva were fully automated and unmanned.

Ore shipments were transported to Mars for industrial refinement.

## Moon: M2

A second moon whose orbital velocity began accelerating unexpectedly.

Fragments from M2 were later deflected toward Earth, contributing to the
global catastrophe.

## Horrox Orbital Station

A large orbital construction platform used to coordinate mining and
probe missions.

It served as a relay between Earth, Mars, and the Minerva mining
complex.

## The Viajero Incident

The spacecraft Viajero attempted to reach the Horrox orbital station
after restoring Minerva operations.

The vessel later malfunctioned and was lost near Horrox shortly before
the M2 destabilization event.
