# Sons of Impact Manifesto

Recovered ideological document attributed to early leadership of the
Sons of Impact.

## Declaration

We are the Sons of Impact.

We were formed because one man understood what others refused to accept:
the meteor was not an accident.

It was a signal.

## Our Belief

Humanity exists inside a cosmic system older than our species.\
The meteor did not simply change the environment.\
It changed the rules of reality.

LightTech discovered fragments of this truth but chose profit over
preparation.

## The Threat

We do not claim to understand the intelligence behind the impacts.

But something reacts when Chronolith is disturbed.

Mining Minerva awakened something.\
Warp travel spreads contamination across space.

Humanity may already have been noticed.

## Our Purpose

We prepare humanity for consequences others refuse to see.

We study the meteor fragments.\
We preserve knowledge.\
We train for a war that may arrive without warning.

## Warning

The universe is not empty.

It is waiting.
