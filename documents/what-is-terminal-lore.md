# What is Terminal Lore?

Terminal Lore is a science fiction universe centered around mysterious
computer devices known as Terminals.

These devices originate in the distant future and were sent backward
through time using anomalous zones created by an extraterrestrial
mineral called Chronolith.

Throughout history the Terminals appear in forms appropriate to their
era using a system known as Contextual Phase Adaptation (CPA). This
allows them to blend into the technology of each period.

The story spans ancient civilizations, pirate legends, World War
discoveries, and a distant future where humanity discovers the planet
Horrox.

Mining operations on Horrox's moon Minerva introduce new Chronolith
materials that enable warp travel, temporal technologies, and
large-scale industrial expansion beyond Earth.

However, the spread of Chronolith through space may have awakened or
alerted an unknown extraterrestrial intelligence.

Terminal Lore explores the consequences of humanity discovering
technologies it does not fully understand while attempting to change its
own future.
