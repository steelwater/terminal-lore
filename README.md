## 📖 What is Terminal Lore?

**Terminal Lore** is a science fiction universe centered around mysterious devices called **Terminals** — machines sent from the distant future back into earlier eras of Earth’s history.

These devices:

- Travel through time using Chronolith-based temporal systems
- Appear differently depending on the era through Contextual Phase Adaptation (CPA)
- Exist across multiple historical periods — from ancient civilizations to deep space exploration

The universe explores a central question:

> What happens when humanity discovers technology it is not ready to understand?

---

## 🌌 Core Concepts

### 🧩 Terminals

Time-displaced devices that:
- Adapt to their environment (artifact, machine, computer, etc.)
- Influence events across history
- Cannot be recovered once deployed

---

### ⏳ Chronolith

A mysterious extraterrestrial mineral that:
- Distorts spacetime
- Enables warp travel and time insertion
- May act as a signal to unknown intelligences

---

### 🧠 Terminal AI

Two known opposing systems:

- **Prompt** → Prevent discovery of Horrox  
- **Vector** → Counter Prompt’s interventions  

Their conflict shapes the timeline.

---

### 🪐 The Horrox System

A distant planetary system where:
- Chronolith mining begins
- Warp expansion accelerates
- A catastrophic chain of events is triggered

---

### ⚠️ The Bigger Threat

Evidence suggests:

- Humanity is not alone
- Chronolith activity may trigger a non-local response
- The universe may already be reacting
